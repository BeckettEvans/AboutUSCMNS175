3. Timeline: A Decade of Change

1862 Town Founded around sawmill operation.
1980 Economic Anxiety as forests deplete.
1982 Revitalization Begins: First 5 murals commissioned.
1983 "The Little Town That Did": Sawmill closes; NYC award won.
Present Global Destination: 44+ murals, 300+ businesses, 400K+ visitors.
